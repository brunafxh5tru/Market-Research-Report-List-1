<p><strong>The Portable Pyrometer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Optical Technology</li><li>Infrared Technology</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The portable pyrometer market is primarily segmented into two types: optical technology and infrared technology. Optical pyrometers measure temperatures by detecting the visible light emitted from hot objects, suitable for high-temperature environments and specific materials. In contrast, infrared pyrometers utilize non-contact sensing to detect thermal radiation, allowing for rapid and accurate temperature readings across various surfaces without requiring direct contact. Both technologies cater to industries such as manufacturing, maintenance, and quality control, enhancing operational efficiency and safety.</p></p>
