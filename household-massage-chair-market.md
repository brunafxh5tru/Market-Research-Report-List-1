<p><p>The household massage chair market features two primary types: rail massage chairs and trackless massage chairs. Rail massage chairs operate on a fixed rail system, enabling a defined range of motion for the rollers, which often results in a more traditional massage experience. In contrast, trackless massage chairs utilize a more flexible mechanism, allowing the rollers to move freely across various areas of the back, providing a customizable and adaptive massage experience. Both types cater to different consumer preferences and needs.</p></p>
