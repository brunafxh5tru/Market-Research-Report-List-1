<p><ul><li>Rapid Diagnostic Test (RDT)</li><li>Enzyme Linked Immunosorbent Assay (ELISA)</li><li>Neutralization Assay</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The COVID-19 IgG/IgM Rapid Test Cassette market is segmented into three main types. Rapid Diagnostic Tests (RDTs) provide quick results using a simple blood sample, offering immediate insights into antibody presence. Enzyme-Linked Immunosorbent Assay (ELISA) is a laboratory technique that quantitatively measures antibodies, requiring specialized equipment and longer processing times. Neutralization Assays assess the functional ability of antibodies to inhibit viral activity, providing critical information for evaluating immune response and vaccine efficacy. Each type plays a vital role in COVID-19 diagnostics.</p></p>
